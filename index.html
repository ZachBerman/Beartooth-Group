Meet the Team

Robert Keith

Managing Principal & Co-Founder

Robert previously worked with Greenbridges LLC (a real estate investment firm), Trident Capital (a Silicon Valley venture capital firm), and Morgan Stanley. Robert earned a BA in Economics from Yale University and an MBA from the Graduate School of Business at Stanford University. Originally from Minneapolis, Minnesota and Cody, Wyoming, Robert enjoys fishing, hiking, riding, rafting, hunting, camping, climbing and virtually anything else outdoors.

Sten Anderson

Financial Advisor

Prior to joining Beartooth, Sten worked with Freestone Capital Management and KPMG. Sten earned his Master’s in Professional Accountancy from Montana State University and his BA in Business Administration from The Master’s College. He is also a Certified Public Accountant. Originally from Bozeman, Montana, Sten is an outdoorsman who enjoys distance running, hunting, snowboarding, mountaineering, and fishing.

Brokerage

We represent buyers, handling rigorous property searches, negotiations, due diligence, valuation analyses and more. Having been a direct investor in the Western property market for the last eleven years, we bring the high level of service demanded by professional investors and buyers.

Project Management

We are a single point of contact for the planning, implementing, and overseeing of large restoration projects. As direct investors for over a decade, we have conducted creek/river and wetland restoration, sustainable timber harvest, environmental clean-up, wildlife management and agricultural operations. Our expertise adds financial, ecological and recreational value.
